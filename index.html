Explanation of ShareableURL.com: ...Unlike other URL shorteners (like tinyURL.com or bit.ly), shareableURL.com offers users a variety of descriptive domains to choose from to shorten & customize their links to make them more transparent, including:
- MeetingURL.com
- EpisodeURL.com
- SongURL.com
- PodcastURL.com
- PlaylistURL.com
- Groupch.at
and more! ShareableURL.com also allows users to shorten their links with their own domain name (like their company domain) if they have one. Futhermore, ShareableURL.com allows to protect their links with reCAPTCHA and/or a password. Lastly, ShareableURL.com provides users with a dashboard with stats like how many clicks their links have gotten.
